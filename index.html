Further Information

Blueberry Analytics price estimate is our best approximation of the expected purchase price for this property in the current market situation. Blueberry Analytics rent forecast is accordingly the market rent we expect. A large number of the recorded characteristics are taken into account as well as data and meta-data collected about the location of the property.

Blueberry Analytics price and rent estimates are determined using an automatic statistical method that combines all available information. According to this forecast, the sales price or rent will most likely be in the range shown. Since purchase and rental prices are freely agreed between the contracting parties, there is no guarantee that a corresponding price will be realized. Legal restrictions, such as the so-called "rent cap", are also not taken into account other than the price action reflected directly from the data.

Data sources

Publicly and privately available sources are used as the data basis for the calibration of the statistical procedure. Several million data records are already available, and the number is rising. We employ 3rd party real estate demographic data vendors, namely Empirica Systems1 and ESRI2 demographics, to keep our database current. Property data and meta-data is updated at each customer report request. Municipality data is updated monthly.

Consideration of property features

All characteristics for which we have sufficient data are taken into account. Features for which little or no data are available cannot be taken into account, just as features that have not been recorded. You can find an overview of the recorded characteristics under "Property data".

Statistical Methodology

Using K-means clustering, regression analysis and machine learning. Minimum 30 comparable data points per valuation. Sensitivity analysis based on all data from the municipality for better generalization.

No market value determination

Please note that this is not a determination of market value in the sense of § 194 BauGB. The procedure used here is purely data-based and does not require any manual adjustments. Nevertheless, it can make sense to have the property appraised by an expert and, if necessary, have an expert report drawn up.
